MetaXar: Cryptocoin Airdrop Announcement

  • Client ProximaX
  • Category Web Design
  • Date February, 2022

During my internship as a UI/UX Designer at ProximaX, I had the opportunity to design the website for MetaXar, a cryptocurrency airdrop announcement platform. Utilizing Adobe XD, I crafted the design and prototype with the aim of creating an engaging and informative user experience. The website's primary goal was to clearly communicate the details of the cryptocurrency airdrop, making it straightforward for users to understand how to participate. I focused on developing a layout that was both visually appealing and functional, ensuring that the information was easily accessible and the site navigation was intuitive.

Collaborating closely with the ProximaX team, I incorporated feedback and refined the design to meet the specific needs of the MetaXar project. The end result was a professional and user-friendly website that effectively conveyed the excitement and potential of the MetaXar airdrop to its audience. This project allowed me to apply and enhance my UI/UX design skills, contributing to a successful launch and promoting user engagement with the MetaXar cryptocurrency platform.
