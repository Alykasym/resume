Payluy: E-Wallet Website Redesign

  • Client Payluy
  • Category Web Design
  • Date March, 2022

I present the website design crafted for "Payluy", a leading e-wallet company based in Cambodia. This project was undertaken during my tenure as a UI/UX Designer intern at ProximaX, where I was tasked with the challenge of revamping Payluy's online presence to enhance user experience and engagement. Leveraging Adobe XD, I developed a user-centric design, focusing on ease of navigation, aesthetically pleasing visuals, and a streamlined user journey. The design not only met but exceeded the client's expectations by embodying the company's vision and values, thus significantly contributing to its digital footprint and user satisfaction.

The design process entailed extensive research on current trends in e-wallet platforms, user behavior analysis, and iterative testing to ensure the interface was intuitive and accessible for all users. By integrating feedback from stakeholders and potential users, I refined the prototype to perfection, emphasizing security features, quick transaction capabilities, and a responsive layout for various devices. This project not only showcases my ability to translate complex requirements into compelling digital experiences but also highlights my dedication to embracing innovative design solutions that drive business growth and user delight.
